About these PCs

The best system for After Effects depends on the specific needs of the user and their video resolution. After Effects utilizes both CPU cores, higher clock speeds and GPUs for specific effects. The requirements vary depending on the resolution of the video content (e.g. HD, 4K, 6K or 8K) and the complexity of functions being used. Functions such as dissolves, warp stabilization, color replacement, and exporting will place more demands on CPU and GPU resources. Ultimately, it's important to assess the specific needs and tasks to determine the best configuration for After Effects.
